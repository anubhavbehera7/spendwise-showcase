SpendWise

Introduction

SpendWise is a budgeting app built to help users track where their money goes, set spending goals, and stay aware of financial habits through clear organization and guided logging. It allows users to record expenses, review categorized spending, and monitor budget progress through visual summaries and straightforward screens.

The app includes Savings Circles, which lets groups work toward shared savings challenges, making it suitable for managing costs during trips, planning group activities, or encouraging collective accountability. SpendWise also provides a dashboard with visual insights, tools for creating and managing budgets, and an integrated chatbot that offers financial tips and helpful guidance.

By combining personal budgeting structure with optional group participation and supportive features, SpendWise creates a simple and flexible experience for anyone looking to manage spending with more confidence.
